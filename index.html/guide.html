⚡ Fast Level Grinding Guide

Level 1 – 50

Start at Starter Island. Complete all bandit quests. Focus on melee or light fruit for fast grinding.

Level 50 – 150

Move to Jungle Island. Farm monkeys and gorillas. Always take quest before defeating enemies for bonus XP.

Level 150 – 300

Go to Desert and Snow Village. Use area damage fruits like Flame or Light. Upgrade stats wisely (Melee + Defense).

Pro Grinding Tips

✔ Always accept quest before fighting.
✔ Use XP boost codes.
✔ Join public servers for faster respawn.
✔ Focus on one main stat build.



Level 300 – 475 (Skylands)

Farm Sky Bandits and Dark Masters. Always take quests before fighting. Light or Buddha fruit recommended.

Level 475 – 700 (Colosseum & Magma Village)

Farm Gladiators and Toga Warriors. Defeat Magma Admiral boss for bonus XP. Complete First Sea at Level 700.

Level 700 – 950 (Second Sea - Kingdom of Rose)

Defeat Raiders, Mercenaries and Swan Pirates. Buddha fruit is very effective here.

Level 950 – 1200 (Green Zone & Graveyard)

Farm Marine Captains and Zombies. Upgrade Melee and Defense stats.

Level 1200 – 1500 (Hot & Cold Island)

Farm Snow Troopers and Magma Ninjas. XP gain is faster in this zone. Reach 1500 to unlock Third Sea.

Level 1500 – 1700 (Port Town)

Farm Pirate Millionaires and Pistol Billionaires. Buddha fruit highly recommended.

Level 1700 – 2000 (Hydra Island)

Defeat Dragon Crew Warriors and Dragon Crew Archers. Focus on Melee + Defense build.

Level 2000 – 2300 (Great Tree)

Farm Forest Pirates and Mythological Pirates. Use AoE damage fruits for faster clears.

Level 2300 – 2500 (Floating Turtle)

Farm Fishman Raiders and Fishman Captains. XP gain is very efficient in this zone.

Level 2500 – 2800 (Sea of Treats)

Defeat Candy Pirates and Chocolate Commanders. Use 2x XP boosts for maximum efficiency. This area gives fastest XP near max level.

Pro Tips for Fast Grinding

✔ Always take quests before fighting.
✔ Use 2x XP boost codes.
✔ Buddha fruit is best for grinding.
✔ Join active servers for faster respawns.
✔ Avoid wasting time on bosses unless needed.
